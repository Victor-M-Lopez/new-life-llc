OUR COMPREHENSIVE REPAIR SERVICES

Expert Repairs to Restore, Protect, and Enhance Your Property.

Get a free quote today!

At New Life Painting Services LLC, we go beyond painting to offer a full suite of repair services that keep your property looking and functioning at its best. Serving Frederick County, Baltimore, and Washington DC, our skilled team is ready to tackle any repair task, big or small. Whether it's patching drywall, fixing exterior surfaces, or restoring structural integrity, we provide reliable, quality repairs that enhance the beauty and longevity of your property.

Residential

Our residential repair services

Drywall Repair and Installation

From small wall dents to large-scale drywall installation, we ensure a seamless, smooth surface that's ready for painting, restoring the look and integrity of your walls.

Water Damage Repair

We identify and repair water-damaged areas in your home, treating both visible damage and underlying issues to prevent mold growth and further deterioration.

Stucco and Plaster Repair

Our team expertly fixes cracks, chips, and holes in stucco or plaster, leaving a flawless finish that blends naturally with your home's aesthetic.

Exterior Surface Repair

From siding to fascia, we repair a variety of exterior surfaces to protect your home from the elements and enhance its curb appeal.

Window and Door Repair

We address broken frames, damaged weather stripping, and more to improve your home's energy efficiency and security.

Our commercial repair services
