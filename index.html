About

Get to know us a bit more

At New Life Painting Services LLC, we specialize in providing top-notch residential and commercial painting services in Frederick County, Washington DC, and Baltimore, Maryland. As a locally-owned and operated business, our team is dedicated to delivering exceptional interior and exterior painting solutions that breathe new life into your spaces. Whether you're a homeowner looking to refresh your living room with a new coat of paint or a property manager needing reliable apartment painting services or drywall repairs, we have you covered.

Our mission is simple: to provide high-quality painting and remodeling services with a focus on customer satisfaction and attention to detail. We believe that a fresh coat of paint can transform not just a room, but an entire home or business. That’s why we use only the best materials and the latest techniques to ensure a flawless finish every time.
