At New Life Painting Services LLC, we believe a beautifully painted home brings new energy and personality into any space. From selecting the perfect color to the final brushstroke, our team is committed to making your vision a reality, using only the highest-quality materials and professional techniques.

Residential

Our residential painting services

Interior Paint

Transform the look and feel of your living spaces with our expert interior painting services. From living rooms and kitchens to bedrooms and bathrooms, we bring each room to life with colors and finishes that match your personal style.

Trim and Baseboard Painting

Our trim and baseboard painting service adds a crisp, polished look to your rooms, providing the perfect finishing touch to any interior space.

Cabinet Painting Refinishing

Breathe new life into your kitchen or bathroom with professionally painted or refinished cabinets. This service offers a budget-friendly alternative to remodeling, achieving a modern, updated look without replacing cabinetry.

Exterior Painting

Enhance your home's curb appeal with a fresh coat of paint! We use high-quality exterior paints designed to withstand Maryland's changing weather, providing both beauty and protection for your home.

Deck and Fence Staining/Painting

Protect and beautify your outdoor spaces with deck and fence staining or painting. Our team uses durable stains and paints that extend the life of your wood surfaces and enhance your home's outdoor appeal.

Popcorn Ceiling Removal

Modernize your home by removing outdated popcorn ceilings. Our team safely and efficiently removes these textures, leaving a smooth, updated surface ready for a fresh coat of paint.
