OUR RELIABLE MAINTENANCE SERVICES

Comprehensive Maintenance Services for Homes and Businesses – Keeping Your Property in Excellent Condition All Year Round.

Get a free quote today!

At New Life Painting Services LLC, we understand that regular maintenance is key to preserving the value, safety, and appearance of any property. Our reliable maintenance services are designed to take the hassle out of property upkeep, so you can enjoy a worry-free environment that always looks its best. From seasonal tune-ups to monthly inspections, we offer flexible maintenance plans tailored to meet the unique needs of both residential and commercial properties in Frederick County, Baltimore, and Washington DC.

Residential

Our residential maintenance repair services

Exterior Painting and Touch-Ups

Keep your home's exterior looking fresh and vibrant with seasonal or as-needed touch-ups to prevent fading, chipping, and other weather damage.

Seasonal Power Washing

Remove dirt, grime, and mildew buildup from surfaces like siding, decks, and driveways with our professional power washing service, perfect for spring and fall cleanup.

Window and Door Caulking

Prevent drafts and improve energy efficiency with regular caulking around windows and doors, which also helps block out moisture and pests.

Gutter Cleaning and Maintenance

Our team clears and maintains gutters to prevent water damage and roof leaks, keeping your home safe and protected from seasonal weather changes.

Deck and Patio Maintenance

Extend the life of your deck and patio with regular staining, sealing, and repairs, ensuring these spaces stay beautiful and safe for use.

Our commercial maintenance services
